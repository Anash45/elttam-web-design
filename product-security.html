PRODUCT SECURITY

elttam's Product Security assessment service helps organisations fortify their products against cyber threats using a code-assisted and threat driven security testing approach. We identify impactful vulnerabilities and weaknesses, then provide tailored guidance to bolster product security and reduce risk.

Advanced security testing to fortify Products against likely threats

Over the years product security practices have changed and evolved. Long gone are the days of a blackbox penetration test prior to a production go-live. Instead, organisations often build a layered security assurance program where they shift security left in the development lifecycle, and then leverage services such as penetration testing and bug bounty programs to provide third-party assurance.

At elttam, we strive to provide advanced penetration testing services to our customers where they need genuine expertise to help uncover security vulnerabilities and help them fortify their products in a pragmatic manner. We do this by providing a collaborative, code-assisted, and threat driven security testing approach that's carefully tailored to meet requirements and provide tangible outcomes.

Our methodologies and guides start with relevant industry standards such as OWASP, CWE, and MITRE ATT&CK, however we extend our approaches to be on the bleeding edge of approaches and tactics across all technology layers. We take a systematic approach by dissecting the architecture of the product, consider its unique threat model, then combine offensive research and security engineering skill-sets to simulate likely threat scenarios and carefully assess attack surfaces along with crafting tailored guidance.
